Your goal is to hunt down The Questing Beast before it drives you insane.
At the start of the game, you may only vote Success. If two "The Questing Beast Was Here" cards were played, you become enraged and may only vote Fail.

  • You start the game paranoid, with 0 sanity points. You win if either a) no "The Questing Beast Was Here" cards were played or b) you become sane, with at least 2 sanity points at the end of the game.
    • Every time you see "The Questing Beast Was Here" card you lose 1 sanity point;
    • The first time you go on a mission with The Questing Beast, you gain 1 sanity point (cancelling out with the previous effect);
    • If there are 3 failed missions, you gain 2 sanity points;
    • If you correctly identify The Questing Beast at the end of the game you gain 3 sanity points. You should attempt this before the Assassination phase, and you will be told if you are correct after Assassination.
