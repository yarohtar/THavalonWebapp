You may play Reversal cards when sent on missions.

  • A mission with one Reversal card is evaluated as follows:
    • If one Fail is required for the mission to fail, the mission outcome is reversed
    • If two Fails are required for the mission to fail then:
      • If no Fail cards were played, the mission succeeds.
      • If a single Fail card was played, the mission fails.
      • If two or more Fail cards were played, the mission succeeds.
  • A mission with two Reversal cards is evaluated as usual, with both Reversals being counted as Success.
